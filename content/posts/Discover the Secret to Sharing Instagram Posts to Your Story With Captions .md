## Steps to Share Instagram Posts to Your Story With Captions

Sharing posts to your story with captions is easy. Here's how to do it:

1. Open the post you want to share.

2. Tap the share button.

3. Select “Add Post to Your Story.”

4. Add a caption.

5. Tap “Share.”
